What and when is National Teacher's Day
in Indonesia?

National Teachers' Day is a day dedicated to respecting, appreciating and celebrating the important role of teachers in forming the younger generation and advancing education.
This day is celebrated in various countries, usually on different dates, as a form of respect for the contribution of teachers in building society and educating the nation's life.
In Indonesia, National Teachers' Day is celebrated every 25 November, coinciding with the birth of the Republic of Indonesia Teachers' Association (PGRI) in 1945.
The purpose of National Teachers' Day in Indonesia is to respect and appreciate the services of teachers, increase public awareness of the importance of the role of teachers in the world of education, provide motivation for teachers to continue to contribute to the intelligent life of the nation, and strengthen relations between teachers, students and society.
